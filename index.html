Where the Art of Combat meets the Science of Learning.

For Martial Artists + Educators + Scientists

Welcome to Martial Methodology, where the art of combat meets the science of learning. My band of misfits and I are going to disrupt the status quo of curriculum and instruction in the martial arts world, and hopefully revolutionize how it's taught while we're at it.

Nothing is off limits here. We discuss motor learning and educational psychology and how it relates to everything from course design to teaching individuals with special needs -- even to coaching high-level athletes. Stay tuned, stay focused, and don't miss a word, because this isn't light reading.

But it's oh so worth it.
