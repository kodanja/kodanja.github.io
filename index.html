Joshua David Peacock

copywriter

I think too hard, fuss over details, and sometimes wear two different-colored socks. Often I’ll waste an entire evening learning about things I don’t know. I’ve put myself into others’ shoes so much I forgot where I put my own.

(Don’t tell anyone, but, one time, I double-dipped in the salsa. At church.)

If you can still tolerate me after all that…well, I guess you could say I’m pretty creative and a good wordsmith.

My ability to empathize with and speak persuasively to anyone -- and do it using a friendly, conversational tone -- makes me valuable to you as a writer
